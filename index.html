About

Who I am, and why I'm here.

  • 2016-2021

    Why am I here?

    I am a senior Accounting student, who started my collage career in graphic design. Because I have created so many things during my time in the school of art, what better place to store some of my best work.    

  • Fall 2016

    A Design Student is Born

    I came to BJU as a greene freshman, wanting to follow a dream to become an architect, to my dismay, BJU doesnt offer that career, so I settled for the next best thing, until an interior design class could be offered the following year.  

  • December 2017

    Transition to Accounting

    It was the November right at the halfway point, where I was starting to plan out my Mid-Career presentation, on why I should continue in the current major, the "Sophomore Presentation". I had just gotten back form a week long university sponsored art trip from NY, and I realized, that I didn't LOVE art the same way the other students did, I wasn't as passionate about the subject, and I was disenchanted with the subject. Especially about having to sell my skills to make an actually living, this was difficult when I didn't see anything worth selling, and when I have to pay off a Degree, I decided to move to something more practical, and far more secure income wise.      

  • May 2021

    The Road Ahead

    Now I'm here, at the end of my Accounting career. It was difficult, some things I didn't know, and never even wanted to know, however it has been the only topic I have felt genuine happiness and joy, when something comes together and everything balances just right. Going forward, Lord willing, I can get a position and build a stable career.
